OSU App Club

A place for you to come learn, work, and even play with mobile applications. We will be working with iOS and Android from start to finish. Members are welcome from any experience level.

There will be some experienced members that are great learning resources:

  • iOS (iPhone/iPad): Chris Vanderschuere
  • Android: Ryley Herrington
  • Titanium (iOS/Android): Nicole Phelps
