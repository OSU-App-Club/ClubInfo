OSU App Club

A place for you to come learn, work, and even play with mobile applications. We will be working primarily with iPhone/iPad apps, but Ryley is going to be there to help with Android development.

Current Notices

Check out the Upcoming Events for some of the things in the works.

How to Join

1 Join the mail list

  • A key way we get in contact with eachother is through the email list. It is very important that you sign yourself up to recieve these messages.
  • Signup on this page: http://lists.oregonstate.edu/mailman/listinfo/iphone

2 Come to a meeting

  • Meeting times haven't been decided for Fall 2013 yet, but there will be an email and this website will be updated. Here is the doodle for the fall term meeting.

Questions

If you ever have any questions, feel free to send the group an email with iPhone@lists.oregonstate.edu or to me (Chris) directly at vandersc@onid.oregonstate.edu
